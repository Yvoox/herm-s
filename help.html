Help

The dashboard has several maps based on the parameters you entered in the menu. Here, we describe each representation based on the input parameters.

- The geographical view allows you to see the key points (restaurants and customers) in their real positions in the map using their latitude and longitude values.

- With the time slot parameter, you can visualize the orders on a given period of the day in order to see when and where the activities are high.

- You can also lighten or densify the visualization by specifying the number of orders you want to see with the third parameter.

- Finally, you can choose the food type order with the last parameter so that you only see the orders of the specified food.

After setting these parameters, click on the button at the bottom of the menu to visualize the results!
