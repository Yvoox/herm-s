Help

The dashboard has two visualizations based on the parameters you entered in the menu. The menu is available by clicking on the three points. Here, we describe each representation based on the input parameters.

- Graphical view (default) allows you to see the key points (restaurants and customers) with a point of view based on orders. Links represent each delivery.

- The geographical view allows you to see the key points in their real positions in the map using their latitude and longitude values.

- With the time slot parameter, you can visualize the orders on a given period of the day in order to see when and where the activities are high.

- You can also lighten or densify the visualization by filtering by the number of orders you want to see, for restaurants and customers.

- Clicking on a point display the number of orders of a customer or a restaurant and his address, based on his longitude & latitude and Google Places API.

After setting these parameters, click on the button at the bottom of the menu to visualize the results! You can restart the initial view with the restart button.
